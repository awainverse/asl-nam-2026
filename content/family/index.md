## Local Attractions

### Amusement parks

- [Diggerland](https://diggerlandusa.com/) (~40 minutes by car)
- [LEGOLAND Discovery Center](https://www.legolanddiscoverycenter.com/philadelphia/) (~30 minutes by car)
- [The Fun Plex](https://thefunplex.com/mount-laurel/) (~30 minutes by car)
- [Clemonton Park](https://www.clementonpark.com/) (~30 minutes by car)
- [Sesame Place](https://sesameplace.com/philadelphia/) (~40 minutes by car)
- [Dutch Wonderland](https://www.dutchwonderland.com/) (~80 minutes by car)

### Zoos, Gardens & Aquariums

- [Philadelphia Zoo](https://www.philadelphiazoo.org/) (~10 minutes by car, ~30 minutes by public transit)
- [Adventure Aquarium](https://www.adventureaquarium.com/) (~20 minutes by car)
- [Longwood Gardens](https://longwoodgardens.org/) (~50 minutes by car)

### Museums

- [Please Touch Museum](https://www.pleasetouchmuseum.org) (~15 minutes by car, ~30 minutes by public transit)
- [Penn Museum](https://www.penn.museum/) (~10 minute walk) 
- [The Academy of Natural Sciences of Drexel University](https://ansp.org/) (~10 minutes by car, ~20 minutes by public transit)
- [The Franklin Institute](https://fi.edu/en) (~10 minutes by car, ~20 minutes by public transit)

### Parks

- Smith Memorial Playground & Playhouse (free admission; a large playground combined with an indoor play house) (~10 minutes by car)
- Kaskey Park (small park on Penn campus with pond with a lot of wildlife; ~15 minute walk)
- Schuylkill River Park (riverside park across from Penn campus; ~20 minute walk)
- Shofuso Japanese Cultural Center (there's a big area for picnics outside the center itself; ~10 minute drive)
- Lemon Hill Park (big open park; ~10 minute drive)
- Sister Cities Splashpad (~10 minutes by car, ~20 minutes by public transit)

### Markets, Food, and Other Attractions

- Reading Terminal Market (~15 minutes by public transit)
- Italian Market (~20 minutes by car, ~40 minutes by public transit)
- [CookieLab](https://welcome.insomniacookies.com/cookielab/) (~20 minutes by car, ~40 minutes by public transit)
- [Chinese Lantern Festival](https://phillychineselanternfestival.com/) (~10 minutes by car, ~25 minutes by public transit)
